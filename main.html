Welcome to the JSPM's Alumni Portal: Bridging the Past, Present, and Future

The JSPM Alumni Portal is an integrated platform aimed at fostering strong connections between alumni, current students, and faculty members of JSPM institutions. This portal serves as a formal network to facilitate professional engagement, knowledge sharing, and collaboration among its members. Through the portal, alumni can update their career information, participate in institutional events, explore professional opportunities, and contribute to the growth of the academic community. It also acts as a bridge for current students and faculty to access mentorship, career guidance, and industry insights from alumni, thereby enhancing their academic and professional development.
